Chicken Makhani

Description

Chicken makhani, also known as butter chicken, is a popular Indian dish featuring tender chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato-based sauce with aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1 1/2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion and cook until softened.
  2. Add minced garlic and ginger paste, stirring until fragrant.
  3. Stir in ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, garam masala, turmeric, and cayenne pepper. Cook for 1-2 minutes to toast the spices.
  4. Add chicken pieces to the skillet and cook until they are no longer pink on the outside.
  5. Pour in tomato puree and heavy cream. Stir to combine.
  6. Reduce heat to low and let simmer uncovered for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Stir in plain yogurt and season with salt to taste.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh cilantro, with rice or naan on the side.
