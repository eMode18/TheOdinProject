Chicken arroz caldo

Description

Chicken arroz caldo is a Filipino rice porridge infused with ginger, garlic, and saffron-like color from turmeric. It's a comforting dish often served during cold weather or when someone is feeling under the weather.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup uncooked jasmine rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 pound boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fish sauce (optional)
  • Sliced green onions for garnish
  • Hard-boiled eggs, sliced, for serving

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion, minced garlic, and sliced ginger. Saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add uncooked rice to the pot and stir to coat with the aromatics.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Once boiling, add the chicken pieces. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Reduce heat to low and let simmer until the rice is cooked and the chicken is tender, about 20-25 minutes.
  5. Adjust seasoning with fish sauce if desired.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with sliced green onions and hard-boiled eggs.
